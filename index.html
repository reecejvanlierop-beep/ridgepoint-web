Who We Are

A Better Model for Musculoskeletal Care

RidgePoint Performance Medicine was founded on a simple belief: people deserve healthcare that is honest, effective, and centered on their goals.

After years of practicing within larger healthcare organizations, we recognized that many healthcare systems face increasing pressure around volume, revenue, and efficiency. While these realities are part of modern medicine, we felt something essential was often being lost: meaningful patient relationships, individualized care, and adequate time to understand a patient's goals.

RidgePoint was created to build a better model — one where trust is earned, outcomes matter, evidence guides decision-making, and every recommendation is made with the patient's best interest in mind.

Mission

We believe people deserve healthcare that is honest, effective, and centered on their goals. We exist to help people stay active through every stage of life by delivering evidence-based musculoskeletal care focused on meaningful outcomes, respecting patients' time and resources, and always doing what is right for every patient.

Vision

To set the standard for musculoskeletal healthcare by earning trust, delivering meaningful outcomes, and helping people stay active through every stage of life.

Founding Principle

Always do what's right for the patient.

"RidgePoint provides evidence-based, non-operative orthopedic and sports medicine care focused on helping patients achieve meaningful outcomes while respecting their goals, time, and resources."

Our Services

Comprehensive MSK Care,
Under One Roof

Our collaborative model brings together sports medicine, rehabilitation, manual therapy, and movement expertise — designed to help patients reduce pain, restore function, and remain active through every stage of life.

Sports Medicine

Physician-led evaluation and diagnosis for all MSK conditions. Every patient begins here — establishing the right diagnosis and individualized care plan before any treatment starts.

Sports Medicine MD / DO

Rehabilitation & Corrective Exercise

Structured rehabilitation, corrective exercise, and strength training to restore movement and build lasting resilience. Focused on meaningful, measurable functional improvements.

Rehab Specialist

Chiropractic & Manual Therapy

Hands-on care to restore joint mobility, reduce pain, and improve biomechanics. Spinal and extremity treatment coordinated within your overall care plan.

Chiropractor / Manual Therapist

Exercise & Performance

Corrective exercise and performance training to build resilience, prevent re-injury, and help you maintain the strength and independence needed to keep doing what matters most.

Exercise Specialist / Rehab Specialist

Interventional & Procedural Care

When conservative care is insufficient, evidence-based procedural options are available — including ultrasound-guided injections and targeted interventions for persistent MSK conditions.

PM&R / Sports Medicine Physician
